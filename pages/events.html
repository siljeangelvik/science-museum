Professor of Aeronautics

On loan from the NEMO Science Museum in Amsterdam, the Energetica exhibition is coming to the Community Science Museum. as we harness them.

From solar energy powering lighting, to ‘Wind Island’ that shows how turbines are able to use and control wind to create power.
